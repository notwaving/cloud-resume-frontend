About

For over a decade I've been working as an actor, and in the last few years became drawn to the world of tech. After attending a huge variety of meetups and conferences I became passionate about the Cloud and DevOps, but I also knew I wanted a foundation in software engineering first. This is how I found myself at places such as Amnesty International and on the BBC's Step Into Tech programme. At the end of last year I became AWS Certified, and decided to progress my journey of upskilling because I feel ready to marry my empathy, communication and teamwork skills with my technical skills and pursue a career in Cloud and DevOps.

This CV is hosted in AWS, inspired by Forrest Brazeal's Cloud Resume Challenge. The challenge aims to teach software development, version control, infrastructure as code, automation, continuous integration and delivery, cloud services and 'serverless', application security, and networking. Here is a blog detailing what I learned while working on this project.
